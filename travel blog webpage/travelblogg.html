Paris | Tokyo | Budapest

Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is known as the "City of Light" and is famous for its art, fashion, and culture. It is home to the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and the beautiful Seine River.

  • Visit the Eiffel Tower
  • Explore the Louvre Museum
  • Take a Seine River Cruise

Tokyo

Tokyo, the capital of Japan, blends modern skyscrapers with historic temples. It is known for its anime culture, advanced technology, and delicious sushi.

  • Visit the Tokyo Tower
  • Explore Shibuya Crossing
  • Eat authentic sushi

Budapest

Budapest, the capital of Hungary, is famous for its historic architecture, thermal baths, and the beautiful Danube River that divides the city into Buda and Pest.

  • Relax in thermal baths
  • Visit the Parliament Building
  • Enjoy a Danube River cruise
