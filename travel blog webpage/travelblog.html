PARIS

Paris, the capital city of France, is one of the most famous and visited cities in the world. Known as the “City of Light”, it is celebrated for its art, fashion, history, and culture. The city is home to iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Champs-Élysées. Paris is also a global hub for gastronomy, offering world-class cafés, bakeries, and fine dining experiences. With its romantic ambiance, charming streets, and rich heritage, Paris attracts millions of tourists each year and is considered a center of inspiration for artists, writers, and dreamers

  • Visit the Eiffel Tower - Go up the tower for breathtaking views of the city.
  • Explore the Louvre Museum -Home to famous artworks like the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo.
  • Take a Seine River Cruise -A relaxing way to see landmarks like Notre-Dame and the city’s bridges

TOKYO

Tokyo, the capital city of Japan, is a dazzling mix of tradition and modernity. Known for its towering skyscrapers, historic temples, neon-lit streets, and unique pop culture, Tokyo offers something for everyone. The city is also a global hub for technology, fashion, and food. From serene gardens to futuristic shopping districts, Tokyo is a city that never sleeps.

  • Explore the Louvre Museum -Home to famous artworks like the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo.
